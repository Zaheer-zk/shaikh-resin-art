Our Story

Made by hand, kept forever

Shaikh Resin Art began with a simple love — capturing the things we never want to fade. A wedding rose, a couple's initials, a verse close to the heart. We pour each piece by hand, layer by layer, until light moves through it like glass.

From tiny letter keychains to preserved-flower keepsakes and Islamic calligraphy, every creation is made to order and made to last. And if you'd rather create your own — we'll teach you how.
